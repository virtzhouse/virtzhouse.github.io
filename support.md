Need help with **VirtzHouse**? There are several ways to get your virtual machines up and running.

## 1. Community & Discussions
For general questions, tips, or sharing your setup, join the community board:
### [d/discussions](https://github.com/virtzhouse/virtzhouse.github.io/discussions/)

This place is particularly interesting for:
*   **Sharing Custom Scripts:** Automated deployment or maintenance scripts.
*   **Host-Guest Interaction:** Optimizing **SSH configs** for seamless host-to-guest interaction.
*   **Setup Guides:** Step-by-step instructions for specific operating systems, i.e. how to install a desktop environment.
*   **Automation:** Specialized configs to let the host execute tasks within your virtual environments. 

## 2. Issue Tracking
If you've encountered a technical bug or want to suggest a new feature, please open an issue in our main repository:
### [Report a Bug](https://github.com/virtzhouse/virtzhouse/issues)

## 3. Direct Contact
For specific inquiries that cannot be handled via public boards, you can reach out via Signal Messenger: virtzhouse_security_tarpods.06

To prevent spam and abuse this address will change regularly.
